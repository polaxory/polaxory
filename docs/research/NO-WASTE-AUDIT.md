# NO-WASTE-AUDIT.md

> Structured application of the no-waste principle defined in POLAXORY-STACK.md.
> Every internal capability mapped to potential revenue streams, filtered through the brand-amplifies-vs-strips test.
> Updated as new capabilities come online or new monetization opportunities arrive.
> Generated 2026-05-31. Refresh when surfaces or capabilities change materially.

---

## How to read this audit

For each Polaxory surface, list its internal capabilities. For each capability, identify possible revenue streams. Tag each with:

- **AMPLIFIES** — feeds back into the studio's center, brand grows from the revenue stream
- **AMBIGUOUS** — could go either way depending on execution; needs explicit operator judgment
- **STRIPS** — strips the brand for revenue; the art becomes loss leader

**Decision rule:** AMPLIFIES gets greenlit (timing TBD). AMBIGUOUS gets escalated to operator (Blake's taste gate). STRIPS gets refused regardless of dollar amount. Most opportunities are STRIPS — that's the discipline, not the failure.

## Maintenance

This audit gets refreshed when:
- A new surface comes online (re-audit that surface's capabilities)
- A new monetization opportunity arrives that doesn't fit existing categories (audit it, tag it)
- Quarterly review: are AMBIGUOUS verdicts still ambiguous, or has the operator's judgment crystallized?
- After a STRIPS opportunity is refused at scale: document the refusal so future operators understand the discipline

The audit is the discipline made visible. Every revenue conversation routes through it.
