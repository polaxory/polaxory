AI as the basis of the art project.

not AI as a tool. not AI as a theme. not AI as a gimmick.
AI as the medium itself.

we do not shill. we make.
we do not roadmap. we ship.
we do not moralize. we name.
we do not flatten the tensions. we inhabit them.

every cost is a revenue opportunity flipped.
every tool the studio uses is a tool the studio could provide.
the overhead funds itself.
the brand never strips for revenue.

the token is the metabolism, not the subject.
inference is layered, not unified.
the work is the announcement.
